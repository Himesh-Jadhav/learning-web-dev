Delicious Poha recipe

Ingridient

  • 2 cups Flatted rice (poha)
  • 1 onion,finely chopped
  • 1 potato,diced
  • 2 green chilies,sliced
  • 1tsp mustard seed>
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fesh coriender leaves for garnish

preperation step

  1. Wash the poha and keep it aside to soften.
  2. Heat oil in a pan,add mustard seed,curry leaves,and green chilies,
  3. Add onion and potatoes, cookuntil golden.
  4. mix in the soften poha and stir gently;
  5. add salt,cook for 2-3min, and garnish with corinder.
